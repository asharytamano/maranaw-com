# MISSION — MARANAW DIGITAL ECOSYSTEM

Our mission is to **collect, digitize, preserve, structure, and share** Maranao cultural, religious, literary, linguistic, and governance-based knowledge through ethical, respectful, and technology-driven platforms.

We commit to:

### ✅ Preservation  
Safeguard endangered Maranao traditions, texts, stories, prayers, laws, and wisdom in digital form.

### ✅ Education  
Create tools and platforms that support learning in:
- Maranao language and literature
- Islamic faith and Tafsir
- Cultural identity and history
- Governance and Shariah

### ✅ Digital Empowerment  
Equip the Maranao people, especially youth and educators, with:
- Access to authentic knowledge
- Modern digital tools
- Culturally-rooted content

### ✅ Platform Development  
Build and maintain:
- Websites
- Mobile applications
- APIs
- Digital archives
- Educational tools

### ✅ Ethical Technology  
Ensure all technology use is guided by:
- Respect for culture and faith
- Authentic sources
- Integrity and transparency
- Community benefit above profit

### ✅ Community & Global Reach  
Connect Maranao communities across the Philippines and the diaspora by:
- Making content accessible online and offline
- Supporting local and international collaboration
- Encouraging cultural pride and involvement

---

In essence, the mission is simple but powerful:

> **To let the Maranao story, faith, and wisdom live on — in code, in content, and in hearts.**
